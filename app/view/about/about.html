About

I'm a Seattle based developer who loves the challenge of building unique, effective user experiences and pushing the limits on design. I want to make the world a more beautiful place and believe the intersection between technology and artistic principles can accomplish this.

I found a passion for software and web development while building a freelance design buisness, and have since aimed to combine challenging technical principles with my previous background in the arts.

I draw inspiration from geometry and patterns found in nature and my artistic work ranges form drawing, jewelry design, acrylic, paper sculpture, lighting design and of course web design. Combined with a technical background and aptitude for programming, I am able to bring visions to life from start to finish.
